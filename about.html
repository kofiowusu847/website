about us and our priorities

The Company was born of the belief that a transformational, formal financial institution was required to make a lasting economic impact on the entrepreneurial poor in Amantin and Kasei. In 1995, Dr. Oduro Boateng initiated a strategy to begin a financial intermediary in Kasei, his hometown to help the rural folk in the vicinity. On4th November, 1996, the BoG granted licenses for Amantin Kasei Community Bank to commence business. The Bankfinally beganits operations on 7th February, 1997 at Amantin

Memberships

The Company is a partner member of the Association of Rural/Community Banks (ARB) Apex Bank. The ARB Apex Bank Ltd is a network of rural and community banks (RCBs) in Ghana financed mainly through the Rural Financial Services Project (RFSP), which is a Government of Ghana project to holistically address the operational bottlenecks of the rural financial sector with the aim of broadening and deepening financial intermediation in the rural areas.There are over hundred and thirty-five rural banks in the ten regions of Ghana. The importance of Rural/Community Banks as providers of financial services to ensure growth in a predominantly agro-based economycannottherefore be over-emphasized.
